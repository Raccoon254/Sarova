THAI CHI BY SAROVA AT 102 MANYANI

A fusion of flavors, delicately balancing sweet & savory elements in artful presentations captivates the eyes before the palate. Such is the allure of Thai cuisine & rich Pan-Asian delicacies embraced with open arms in the vibrant heart of Kenya.
Our appreciation for this nuanced and layered experience inspires us to bring you, Thai Chi by Sarova, the latest dining experience located at 102 Manyani East Road, Lavington. We celebrate the diversity & complexity of rich culinary traditions, each dish telling a story shared by both Kenyan & Thai cultures. Presenting an extraordinary dining experience, the restaurant is set amidst tranquil surroundings, harmoniously blending natural tropical aesthetics with elegant décor & design – Zen, to say the least. Private dining, a garden bar & lounge, and outdoor sitting spaces await every discerning guest.
